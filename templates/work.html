Selected Programs
Digital Exchange Program

Our flagship 12-week, online cultural exchange program to foster cross-cultural understanding and global citizenship.

We match students with a peer from a different country based on language interests, and place them into an online “host family” on problem-area interests. We recruit and train one college-aged student to facilitate each family, which has ~10 students. Our facilitators not only contribute to a vibrant community of teaching and learning, they create and hold safe space for dialogue. Each cohort serves from 150-300 students, and has 15-30 facilitators.

During the program, students meet weekly 1-1 with their partner, and 1:small group with their family. Every week, there’s a big “kickoff” lesson delivered by Project Exchange staff. Students complete and receive feedback on corresponding assignments around culture, storytelling, global citizenship, and community action. Most importantly, there’s a big Slack community, with daily reflection prompts for folks to get to know each other!

Impact Incubator

An 8-week program to help Digital Exchange Program alumni turn what they’ve learned into powerful, creative community action.

We help Digital Exchange Program graduates identify key insights from their experience, and turn these insights into community action projects that addresses a real problem they care about.

Impact Incubator Fellows are matched with a peer mentor: another young person who has launched a successful community action project from start to finish. Fellows participate in weekly workshops and assignments that guide them through their project, covering topics like community mapping, storytelling, fundraising, mobilizing others, and measuring impact. They also receive a mini-grant to help them get started!
